After the Bell

You came over after work… and she's already waiting for you.

The apartment is warm. The day is finally over. And she's been here the whole time — patient, soft, present — saving the best version of the evening just for you.

Digital, 2024 · Available Light

I

I

You walk through the door exhausted from work and find Joyce leaning against the table already smiling at you like she's been counting the minutes. She shifts her hips slowly and watches your reaction with amused patience, fingers lightly gripping the edge of the table while she says, "Long day?"

II

II

She saved you a seat beside her by the window. As you sit down, she smooths her skirt against her thighs and tilts her head with that soft expression that instantly takes the stress out of your chest. She keeps stealing little glances at you while pretending to act casual.

III

III

Back at the apartment, she settles onto the carpet near you and slowly slides closer while talking about her day. Her movements are relaxed and familiar — the kind that only happen when someone feels safe with you. Every now and then she looks up with a teasing little smile just to see if you're still watching her.

IV

IV

You catch her standing near the curtain in the fading light, quietly waiting for you to come closer. She brushes her hair over one shoulder and turns slowly toward you, smiling softly like she already knows exactly what kind of mood you walked in carrying.

V

V

She sits comfortably on the floor while you unwind from the day, listening carefully as you talk. Joyce folds her hands neatly in her lap, nodding gently, giving you that rare feeling that someone is completely present with you in the moment.

VI

VI

She stretches lazily on the carpet after hours of waiting for you to get home, smiling when she notices you staring. Her movements are slow and natural, comfortable in the quiet intimacy of the room, like the evening finally starts once you arrive.

VII

VII

You distracted her while she was fixing her hair. She pauses halfway through, turning toward you with that playful look that says she likes being watched by you more than she'll admit out loud. She gently runs her fingers through her hair and stays there for a second longer than necessary.

VIII

VIII

She sits near the window while the room fills with warm evening light, absentmindedly touching her neck while listening to you move around the apartment. The smile she gives you feels less like posing and more like quiet relief that you're finally home.

IX

IX

The apartment is calm now. Joyce sits quietly in the sunlight, braid resting over her shoulder while she watches you with tired but affectionate eyes. She barely says anything — just smiles softly, shifts a little closer, and lets the silence between you feel comfortable.

X

X

You finally make it into the bedroom and Joyce is already sitting on the edge of the bed waiting for you, hands resting behind her as she leans back slightly with that patient little smile. She watches your tired expression soften the moment you see her, gently swinging one leg while asking if you want to forget about the outside world for a while.

XI

XI

She stretched out on the carpet while waiting for you to finish showering, propping herself up on one elbow while casually talking to you across the room. Every so often she slowly adjusts her stocking and smiles like she knows exactly how distracting she's being. The whole room feels warm, quiet, and lived-in.

XII

XII

You walk back into the room and find Joyce standing near the fireplace with soft amber light reflecting across her skin. She slowly traces her fingertips along the table beside her and holds eye contact just long enough to make the silence feel charged. Her expression says she's happy you're finally here.

XIII

XIII

She's lying across the bed scrolling through her phone while waiting for you, but the second you enter the room she stops paying attention to it completely. Joyce rolls gently onto her side, resting her head against the pillow while watching you unpack your things like this quiet little routine matters more to her than she admits.

XIV

XIV

You find her stretched comfortably across the bed, arms above her head, completely relaxed now that you're home. She shifts slightly against the sheets and gives you that slow affectionate smile that says the night can finally begin. The room is calm, warm, and intimate — like she's been saving this version of herself just for you.
