Vacation Walk

Candid · Outdoor · 2023

No itinerary, no destination — just her and the afternoon light. These are the kind of moments that happen between the planned ones, the ones you remember longer.

Film, 2023 · Outdoor

I

She sets the pace and you follow. That's always been the natural order of things.

II

The light finds her the way it always does — like it made a prior arrangement.

III

Mid-step, mid-thought. Some people are most beautiful when they don't know they're being seen.

IV

She pauses at something you didn't notice. That's her gift — she sees the world a beat before anyone else does.

V

The walk has no destination and that suits her perfectly. She's always been better at the journey than the arrival.

VI

She turns and the whole afternoon reorganizes itself around her face.

VII

End of the walk. She's already thinking about the next one, and you're already hoping you're invited.
