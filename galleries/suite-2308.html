Suite 2308
The Tokyo Hours

Forty floors above the city. Silk on the sheets, the skyline in every window, Tokyo Tower burning orange in the dark. She moves through the suite like she's always belonged here — unhurried, luminous, entirely her own.

This is what it looks like when the city is yours and she is too.
